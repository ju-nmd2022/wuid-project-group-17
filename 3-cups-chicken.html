Three Cups Chicken

Ingredients

4 servings

0.5kg Chicken wings or Chicken legs
1 cup of Soya sauce
1 cup of Beer
1 cup of Sugar
5 peeled garlic cloves
5 Basil leaves

Steps

1.Cut the chicken into pieces and rub them with the baking soda. Set aside for 10 minutes before rinsing the chicken off with water. Make sure the baking soda is completely rinsed off. Pat dry the chicken pieces and set aside. This is an optional step.
2.Heat up a clay pot on high heat and add the sesame oil. Add the garlic, and stir-fry until aromatic. Add in the chicken and do a few quick stirs.
3.Add the soy sauce, beer, sugar and continue to stir-fry the chicken. Cover the lid, lower the heat and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
4.Add the basil leaves and stir well with the chicken, dish out and serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 273g
Fat: 11g
Sugar: 5g
Protein: 24g
